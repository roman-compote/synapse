## Course Overview

Comprehensive 4-week course focused on deploying production-grade Gen AI and Agentic AI systems at enterprise scale. Emphasizes scaling patterns, monitoring, enterprise rollout, and multi-cloud deployment with deep focus on AWS.

**Course Description:**
"Deploy Gen AI & Agentic AI at Scale - Master production deployment across AWS, GCP, Azure, and Vercel with enterprise-grade practices."

**Learning Philosophy:**
Infrastructure challenges are "where the learning happens" - students engage in independent research and troubleshooting while maintaining critical verification practices.
