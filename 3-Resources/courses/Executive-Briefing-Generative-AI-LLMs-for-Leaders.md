## Course Overview

Concise executive briefing for business leaders, strategists, and decision-makers. Published May 2025, designed to provide strategic frameworks for Gen AI/LLM strategy, commercial decision-making, and leading AI initiatives with measurable impact.

**Course Description:**
"Generative AI 2025 Executive Briefing: LLMs for Leaders - Strategic playbook for strategy, governance, and ROI in Generative AI."

**Learning Philosophy:**
Practical, actionable frameworks over technical depth. Three distinct toolkits for strategy, cross-functional decisions, and end-to-end AI delivery.
